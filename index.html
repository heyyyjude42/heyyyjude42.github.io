Hi, I'm Julie!

I currently study computer science at Brown University, with a focus on human-computing interaction, visual design, and education. Hardly a day ever passes without thinking about my next project, whether it's an app to supplement my never-ending escapades in Dungeons and Dragons, or to help quiz bowl moderators keep track of questions and scores.

Since teaching myself Adobe InDesign at the age of fourteen, I've always known I wanted to spend my life building and creating. I'm also a big fan of Avatar: The Last Airbender, Nintendo games, and breadmaking.
